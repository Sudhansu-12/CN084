Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, yogurt, and water to form a smooth batter. Ensure there are no lumps.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and slowly add the gram flour-yogurt mixture to the pan, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Add curry leaves and bring the mixture to a gentle boil. Cook on low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the kadhi thickens and the raw taste of gram flour disappears.
  7. Meanwhile, cook rice separately as per your preference.
  8. Once the kadhi is cooked and thickened, remove it from heat.
  9. Serve hot kadhi over cooked rice.
  10. Enjoy your comforting and flavorful Kadhi Chawal!
