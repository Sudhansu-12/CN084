Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, skin removed and cleaned
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together yogurt, lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, turmeric powder, garam masala, chili powder, ground cinnamon, and salt to form a marinade.
  2. Make deep incisions in the chicken using a sharp knife.
  3. Apply the marinade generously all over the chicken, making sure to get it into the incisions.
  4. Cover and refrigerate the marinated chicken for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  5. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  6. Place the marinated chicken on a wire rack set over a baking tray.
  7. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and charred on the outside.
  8. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges.
  10. Serve hot with naan bread, rice, or your favorite side dishes.
