Ingredients

  • 10-12 chicken wings, cleaned and tips removed
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken wings with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  2. In another bowl, mix beaten egg, all-purpose flour, and cornstarch to make a batter. The batter should have a medium consistency, neither too thick nor too runny.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or pot.
  4. Dip each marinated chicken wing into the batter, ensuring it's well coated.
  5. Gently drop the coated chicken wings into the hot oil and fry until they are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the fried chicken wings from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with lemon wedges.
  8. Enjoy your delicious and crunchy Chicken Lollipops!
