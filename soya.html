Ingredients

  • 200 grams soya chaap (soy protein chunks)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan and add the soya chaap. Boil for 5 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix together yogurt, ginger garlic paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, turmeric powder, garam masala, chili powder, and salt to form a marinade.
  3. Cut the boiled soya chaap into smaller pieces and coat them with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the marinated soya chaap pieces and cook until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney, onion rings, and lemon wedges.
