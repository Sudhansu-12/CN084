Ingredients

  • For Paratha (Wrap):
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • For Filling:
  • 1 cup boneless chicken, cooked and shredded
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • For Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons mint chutney
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Paratha (Wrap):
  2. In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour, salt, and enough water to form a smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a thin circle (paratha). Cook the parathas on a hot griddle or tawa using oil or ghee until golden brown spots appear on both sides. Keep them aside.
  4. Prepare the Filling:
  5. Heat oil in a pan. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  6. Add sliced tomatoes and bell peppers. Cook until they are soft.
  7. Add shredded chicken, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the flavors combine.
  8. Prepare the Sauce:
  9. In a bowl, mix yogurt, mint chutney, tomato ketchup, and salt to taste. Stir well to combine.
  10. Assemble the Kathi Rolls:
  11. Take a cooked paratha and spread a spoonful of the prepared sauce evenly over it.
  12. Place a portion of the chicken filling in the center of the paratha.
  13. Roll the paratha tightly, sealing the edges to form a Kathi Roll.
  14. Repeat the process with the remaining parathas and filling.
  15. Serve hot Kathi Rolls with extra sauce and enjoy!
